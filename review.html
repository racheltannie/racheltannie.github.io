Here are some books I recently read!

How the Penguins Saved Veronica

by Hazel Prior

This is a story about a stubborn and negative 85 year old woman named Veronica, who's decided to reconnect with her only living relative, who is a long lost grandson; and was also inspired by a documentary to pay a trip to antartica's colony of penguins.

Range

by David Epstein

Through case studies, anecdotes and statistics, Epstein seems to suggest that range can be culminated in state of mind rather than experience alone. Traits like open mindedness, scientific curiosity, challenge one's own ideas - relevant for a polarising world.

Im Thinking of Ending Things

by Iain Reid

An amazing thriller - almost or more engaging than the Netflix adaptation. Kept me at the edge of my seat! Enjoyed the chat and monologues.
